Adds callout blocks after paragraphs containing links to other posts from the same blog.

== Description ==

An interesting way to increase user engagement on your site, Automatic Internal Links will automatically add callout blocks between paragraphs in your content, encouraging readers to view posts that were linked to in the preceding paragraph.
